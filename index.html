Fairmont Le Château Montebello

Montebello, Quebec

Fairmont Le Château Montebello Golf Club is recognized today for its exciting and challenging course layout. Designed by Stanley Thompson in 1929, this golf course remained the private domain of the prestigious Seigniory Club until 1970. Still today, it is amongst the best public courses in Quebec. The course is also designated as a Certified Audubon Cooperative Sanctuary, since 1998.

The Fairmont Le Manoir Richelieu Golf Club is home to 27 world class holes of golf. Renowned architect Darrell Huxham managed to showcase the natural beauty and esthetics of the Charlevoix region.
